Grimmchild is a charm that can be picked up in Hollow knight, by summoning the Grimm Troupe and getting the charm from Troupe Leader Grimm. You can upgrade the charm by defeating the Grimm Troupe's memberes scattered throughout Hallownest, and picking up their flames.
